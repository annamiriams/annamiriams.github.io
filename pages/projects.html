Phoebe Light

Phoebe Light is a journal of poetry, poetics, criticism, and adjacent ventures. It takes its name from the great Alice Notley’s poem “Phoebe Light.” Built with Python, Django, and PostgreSQL, Phoebe Light aims to combine the aesthetics of online poetry journals with the functionality of a submission manager.
